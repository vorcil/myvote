The value of MyVote

Security & Accessibility

MyVote ensures inclusivity of the greatest possible number of electors by adhering to W3C and Government standards.

Location & Convenience

By providing ubiquitous access from any internet enabled device, accessibility and mobility issues are addressed.

Up to Date

With key dates and reminder facility our busy electors should easily find the small amount of time required to register their choice.

Feedback and data visuals
